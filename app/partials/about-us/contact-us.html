Temple Residents

Partha Das

PUJARI

Partha Das was born in Kolkata, West Bengal. He joined The International Society for Krishna Consciousness (ISKCON) after reading the "Bhagavad-Gita As It Is" written by His Divine Grace A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami Prabhupada. Srila Prabhupada's Gita answered many of the questions that Partha Das had been asking.

The lessons from Srila Prabhupada that have made the greatest impact on Partha Das's life are the explanation and the significance of the four regulative principles, and the power of the Maha Mantra. Partha Das is a disciple of ... Swami, who gave him initiation in .... He enjoys listening to the lectures of ... Swami, because the lectures are very inspiring.

Partha Das is the "handyman" of the ISKCON Columbus temple. He provides such services as painting, shopping, cleaning, fixing different items around the temple, cooking for the Deities and the devotees. Presently Partha Das serves as a certified temple Kitchen Manager and he is on the Executive Committee for the ISKCON Columbus temple. He contributes to the long term planning for the temple as well as making sure things go smoothly on a day to day basis.
